THE STORY

Welcome to Vault + Sake, San Marcos' premier nightlife destination right on the historic downtown square. We've combined two unique concepts under one roof-The Vault's energetic club atmosphere and Sake Bar & Bistro's sophisticated lounge experience.

From legendary DJ sets in our Boiler Room to intimate bottle service experiences, plus a diverse food menu served all day and night-we've become the go-to destination for anyone looking for an unforgettable time in San Marcos.
